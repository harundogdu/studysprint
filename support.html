Frequently Asked Questions

Does the app work offline?

Yes. Study Sprint Timer is offline-first.

How do streaks work?

Complete at least one study session on a given day to keep your streak going.

How do I change the theme?

Open Settings then Theme and pick any color you like.

How do I remove ads?

Upgrade to Premium from the app.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings then Restore Purchases with the same Apple ID.

Is there an Apple Watch app?

Yes. Install the Watch app to start sprints from your wrist; sessions sync back to your iPhone automatically.

Can I save sessions to Apple Health?

Yes. Turn on "Write to Apple Health" in Settings; completed sessions appear in Health as Mindful Minutes.

How do I delete my data?

All data is stored locally; clear it from Settings or uninstall the app.
