Privacy Policy

How Study Sprint Timer handles your data.

Last updated: July 23, 2026

Overview

Study Sprint Timer ("the app") is an offline-first focus and Pomodoro study timer. We built it to respect your privacy. The app does not require an account and does not ask for your name, email, or any personal details to work.

Information We Collect

We (the developer) do not collect or store your personal data on any server. All of your app data is stored locally on your device only. If you use the Apple Watch app, your data syncs directly between your iPhone and Watch and never reaches us.

Advertising & Third-Party Services

The free version of the app displays ads through Google AdMob. Google may collect and process certain information to deliver and measure ads. This processing is performed by Google, not by us.

  • We use Google's User Messaging Platform to request your consent where required.
  • On iOS, we show Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt. If you decline, the app still works fully.
  • Learn more in Google's Privacy Policy.

Purchasing Premium removes all ads.

Purchases

Subscriptions and purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We never receive or store your payment information. See Apple's Privacy Policy.

Apple Health (Optional)

If you turn on Apple Health sync, the app writes your completed focus sessions to Apple Health as Mindful Minutes. Only the session duration is written; the app reads nothing from Health. Your Health data stays on your device under Apple's Health privacy protections, and you can turn this off anytime in iOS Settings → Health.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information. We do not share your locally stored study data with anyone.

Your Choices

  • Manage tracking via iOS Settings.
  • Reset your advertising identifier in iOS Settings.
  • Remove ads entirely by purchasing Premium.
  • Delete all local data from the app's Settings, or by uninstalling the app.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

Contact

Questions? Contact us at support@studysprinttimer.com.
