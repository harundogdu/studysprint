Frequently Asked Questions

Does the app work offline?

Yes. Study Sprint Timer is offline-first — no account or internet connection is required to run study sessions and track your progress.

How do streaks work?

Complete at least one study session on a given day to keep your streak going. Miss a day and the streak resets — a gentle nudge to stay consistent.

How do I change the theme?

Open Settings → Theme and pick any color you like. On supported devices, the home-screen app icon updates to match your selected theme.

How do I remove ads?

Upgrade to Premium from the app to enjoy an ad-free experience and unlock extra themes.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings → Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.

How do I delete my data?

All data is stored locally. You can clear everything from Settings → Clear Data, or by uninstalling the app.
